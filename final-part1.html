About Us

Table4Four was created by our desire for married couples to find other, compatible, married couples to go on double dates! We are committed to helping you find other couples that can help grow and encourage your own relationship through fellowship. Table4Four matches based on compatibility of interest and worldview. Don't just take our word for it; check out this article from the Huffingtion Post!

How does Table4Four match married couples with other married couples?

When you sign up for Table4Four you and your spouse will complete a 75 question basic questionnaire. This questionnaire will be the base that Table4Four uses to find other couples with similar interests, personalities and worldviews as you and your spouse. The answers to this questionnaire are solely used to match you and your spouse with other couples. No one will see your responses to the questionnaire.

What's Next?

After taking the questionnaire you and your spouse will create a profile for other couples to view. This can be customized by you! Simply answer a few basic questions such as: ages of spouses, years married, current location along with details of what you are seeking (i.e. peer couples, couples to be mentored by or couples you could mentor). You will also be able to share more about you and your spouse so other couples can get to know you and compare compatibility based off your profile.

Table4Four will continually search to find other couples that are compatible to you and your spouse. Daily, Table4Four will send you profiles of compatible couples. You will have the option to extend a message if you are interested by their profile.
